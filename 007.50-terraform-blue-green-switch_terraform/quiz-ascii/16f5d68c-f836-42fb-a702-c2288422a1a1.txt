HOW DO YOU VERIFY PODS

Command:
kubectl get pods --show-labels

Output Example:
+-------------------+--------------------------------+
| Pod Name          | Labels                         |
+-------------------+--------------------------------+
| diner-a-abc123    | app=diner-a,version=blue       |
| diner-b-def456    | app=diner-b,version=green      |
+-------------------+--------------------------------+

Interpretation:
- diner-a pods = blue (currently serving)
- diner-b pods = green (on standby)
