WHAT COMMAND ACTIVATES BLUE

Command:
terraform apply -var='active_color=blue'

Blueprint:
+------------------------------------+
| Ingress → diner-blue Service → Pods A |
+------------------------------------+

Execution flow:
1. Terraform reads variable active_color=blue
2. Updates ingress.yaml to point to blue service
3. Applies Kubernetes manifests

Result:
Customers are routed to the blue diner again.
